❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with Bansal Coders.

How long does a typical project take? ▼

It depends on complexity. A simple landing page takes 1–2 weeks. A mobile app typically takes 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline during the discovery phase.

How much does a project cost? ▼

Costs vary by scope. Websites start from ₹15,000, mobile apps from ₹50,000, and utility tools from ₹10,000. Contact us for a free, detailed quote.

Do you sign an NDA? ▼

Absolutely. We sign NDAs before any discovery call at no charge. Your intellectual property and project details are completely safe with us.

Who owns the source code? ▼

You do. Upon final payment, all source code, assets, and IP are fully transferred to you. We provide complete documentation for handover.

What's your development process? ▼

We follow an agile methodology with 2-week sprints. You'll receive regular demos, progress updates, and have a dedicated point of contact throughout.

Do you offer post-launch support? ▼

Yes! Every project includes 1 month of free post-launch support. We also offer flexible monthly maintenance plans for ongoing care.
